## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, and it also applies when an individual is representing the project or its community in public spaces.

---

## Guidelines for Maintainers

1. **Be impartial** - Consider both sides fairly
2. **Be prompt** - Respond to reports quickly
3. **Be confidential** - Protect reporter privacy
4. **Be proportionate** - Match response to severity
5. **Be transparent** - Explain decisions clearly

---

## Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior

- First violation: Warning with explanation
- Second violation: Temporary ban (1-3 months)
- Third violation: Permanent ban from project
